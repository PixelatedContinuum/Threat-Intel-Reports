THE HUNTER'S LEDGER - BRAND KIT
================================

Brand colors
  Dark square / dark text : #161B22
  Card / nav background    : #0D1117
  Accent blue              : #58A6FF  (gradient to #1F6FEB on the rule bar)
  Border                   : #30363D
  Light text               : #F0F6FC / #FFFFFF
Typeface: Space Grotesk (SIL OFL) - https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Space+Grotesk

icon/  - the "HL" monogram (square app icon / avatar / favicon)
  hl-icon-1024..16.png   square PNGs (transparent corners). Use 400 or 512 for
                         social profile pictures, 180 for Apple touch.
  favicon.ico            multi-size icon (16/32/48/64/128/256).
  hl-icon-glyph-512-transparent.png  just the blue HL strokes, no square
                         (for watermarks / overlays on your own background).

wordmark/  - the "THE HUNTER'S LEDGER" lockup (no icon)
  wordmark-light.png     light text, transparent  -> place on DARK backgrounds.
  wordmark-dark.png      dark text, transparent    -> place on LIGHT backgrounds.
  wordmark-on-dark-bg.png  light text on the brand dark card -> ready to drop in.

logo-lockup/  - icon + wordmark side by side (full logo for headers/signatures)
  logo-horizontal-light.png        transparent, for dark backgrounds.
  logo-horizontal-dark.png         transparent, for light backgrounds.
  logo-horizontal-on-dark-bg.png   on the brand dark card, ready to use.

Notes
  - Some platforms crop avatars to a circle; the HL stays within the safe area.
  - PNGs are high-resolution - scale DOWN for crisp results, avoid scaling up.
  - For email signatures, the -on-dark-bg or -dark (on light) variants render
    reliably regardless of the reader's theme.
